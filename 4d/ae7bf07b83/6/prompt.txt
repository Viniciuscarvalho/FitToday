# Fix: Add Terms of Use & Privacy Policy links to Paywall (App Store Rejection)

## Context

Apple rejected the app because the subscription paywall (`OptimizedPaywallView.swift`) does not include functional links to Terms of Use (EULA) and Privacy Policy. Apple requires all auto-renewable subscription flows to include:

- Title of auto-renewing subscription
- Length of subscription
- Price of subscription
- **Functional links to Privacy Policy and Terms of Use (EULA)**

The paywall currently only has a text disclaimer ("A assinatura é renovada automaticamente...") in the `legalFooter` section (line 300-308) with **no clickable links**. The links already exist in `LegalSection.swift` and `AppInfoFooter.swift` pointing to `https://fittoday.app/terms` and `https://fittoday.app/privacy`.

## Plan

### 1. Add Terms & Privacy links to the paywall's `legalFooter`

**File:** `FitToday/Presentation/Features/Pro/OptimizedPaywallView.swift` (lines 300-308)

Update the `legalFooter` computed property to add the Terms and Privacy links below the existing auto-renewal disclaimer text. Reuse the same URLs already used in `AppInfoFooter.swift`:

```swift
private var legalFooter: some View {
    VStack(spacing: FitTodaySpacing.xs) {
        Text("paywall.legal.auto_renew".localized)
            .font(FitTodayFont.ui(size: 11, weight: .medium))
            .foregroundStyle(FitTodayColor.textTertiary)
            .multilineTextAlignment(.center)

        HStack(spacing: FitTodaySpacing.md) {
            Link("paywall.legal.terms".localized, destination: URL(string: "https://fittoday.app/terms")!)
            Text("•").foregroundStyle(FitTodayColor.textTertiary)
            Link("paywall.legal.privacy".localized, destination: URL(string: "https://fittoday.app/privacy")!)
        }
        .font(FitTodayFont.ui(size: 11, weight: .medium))
        .foregroundStyle(FitTodayColor.brandPrimary)
    }
    .padding(.horizontal, FitTodaySpacing.sm)
}
```
